TeamSynch!

TeamSynch is a Web Platform to help synchronize task amongst group members. TeamSynch will allow you to not only view the tasks you have been assigned but as well as tasks your team members have been assigned as well. This is so that when you do need to wait for the development of another task before you can continue further in your own task you will be able to see when you can get your process resumed.
